Juba Chamber of Commerce

Website Site Plan

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a digital resource hub for businesses operating in Juba and the surrounding region. The site will provide information on local economic events, host networking opportunities, maintain a reliable business directory, and advocate for commercial growth. It aims to support local entrepreneurs, attract investors, and foster a strong business community.

Target Market

Local business owners, regional entrepreneurs, corporate investors, and community members looking for local services in Juba.

Site Goals

  • Improve member engagement by providing accessible networking schedules and resources.
  • Attract new business investments to the area by showcasing local economic potential.
  • Establish the Chamber as the trusted, central authority for local business matters and directories.

User Personas

1. The Local Entrepreneur

Name: Emmanuel

Profile: A 32-year-old business owner who runs a commercial printing, web development, and graphic design shop in the city. He is looking for B2B (business-to-business) networking opportunities, resources to scale his production, and a directory to advertise his services to corporate clients.

2. The Corporate Executive

Name: Gatluak

Profile: A 45-year-old executive at an international logistics firm. His company is looking to expand operations into the region and needs reliable partnerships, economic data, and connections with local government and business leaders.

3. The New Resident

Name: Deng

Profile: Recently relocated to the area for work. He is looking for a trusted directory of local services, community events, and reputable businesses to fulfill his day-to-day needs.

Scenarios

  • A local print shop owner wants to join the chamber to network with larger corporations. They visit the site to view membership tiers (Non-profit, Silver, Gold) and apply online.
  • An international executive is researching the local market and visits the site to find data on the local business environment and contact information for chamber leadership.
  • A community member needs a trusted contractor and uses the Chamber's Business Directory to find a vetted, highly-rated local business.

SEO Plan

  • Keywords: Juba business directory, local commerce Juba, Central Equatoria economic growth, networking events, business resources.
  • Verify the Chamber's physical address on Google Business Profile.
  • Encourage all Silver and Gold members to link back to the Chamber website from their own company sites to build domain authority.
  • Implement Google Analytics on all pages to track visitor demographics and directory usage.

Design Brief

  • Primary Color: #0F47AF (Navy Blue - represents trust, corporatism, and professionalism)
  • Secondary Color: #FCDD09 (Gold - represents wealth, quality, and premium membership)
  • Background Color: #F4F4F4 (Light Gray - for clean, readable content areas)
  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray/Black - for high contrast readability)
  • Font Family: 'Roboto', sans-serif
